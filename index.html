WHO IS ELIGIBLE?

The scholarship is open to girls aged 13-18 that are able to attend one of our scheduled courses. You do not need any previous coding experience to apply. The scholarship is only open to residents of the United States.

DO I NEED TO BRING MY OWN COMPUTER?

If you have a laptop at home that you can bring in we encourage you to do so. If not, we'll have computers available for you to use during the course.

HOW WILL SCHOLARS BE SELECTED?

Entries will be judged based on their application submission. Applications close on Sunday, May 7th at 11:59pmEST. Applicants should expect to hear back in May.

DO I HAVE TO TAKE THE CLASS THIS SUMMER?

Yes. If you are selected and unable to attend the class the scholarship will be awarded to another participant.

DOES WHERE I LIVE MATTER?

Yes. Scholarships are open to United States residents who live within the metro area of their selected city and meet all other eligibility requirements.

DO I NEED EXPERIENCE TO APPLY?

Definitely not! Whether you've never written a line of code or you're a full-fledged hacker, the scholarship is for anyone who's passionate about learning to code.

DO I NEED TO PROVIDE MY OWN TRANSPORTATION?

It is expected that you provide your own transportation to and from class each day, however, transportation can be provided on an as-needed basis in areas where applicable

WHAT ABOUT FOOD?

Participants are provided with lunch during class days as well as snacks and beverages throughout camp
